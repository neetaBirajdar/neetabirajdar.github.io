There are two ways in German to speak to people, the formal way which applies to the places like Offices, restaurants, etc and there is the informal way which can be used with Friends and Family.

In this blog, we will see the questions which people normally ask while the introduction and answer them formally and informally.

Meeting someone new is always awesome. These are some of the question which help you know the other person better with basic questions and easy possible answers.
